WordPress Affiliate Marketing Plugin Sticks and Monetizes Content Related Products on Blogs and Websites

== Description ==

StickyZon for WordPress allows you to easily add targeted Amazon products based on the content without touching a piece of code. This amazing plugin is just like a set and forget system that will automatically add most popular products from Amazon to your blog posts that are related to your post topic. 

As these products have similarity with the post content your visitors are already looking for, the chances are higher for them to take a look at these products and buy from Amazon! As they are referred through your affiliate link, you make easy commissions without any work!

== Installation ==

1. Upload `StickyZon-Lite` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I get Amazon Secret and Api? =

If you do not have an Amazon Affiliate account, please sign up and go to: 

https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/gp/advertising/api/detail/your-account.html 

where you can grab your Amazon Product Advertising API and Secret keys.

= Why there is no product added after my post content? =

StickyZon Lite searches products based on your post tags, categories and pre-defined search keywords By That order. 

If no product is found using these, the StickyZon Lite section after the post will be blank. 

Also please check if you have selected the correct Amazon Department from StickyZon settings, (Do not choose ‘ALL’, you must choose a category) .
